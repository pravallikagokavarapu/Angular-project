Instructions to be followed by candidate before taking the exams.

  • The exam is monitored by the proctor online using the webcam.
  • The participant should not navigate away from the website.
  • The participant should not move away from the webcam as well.
  • In case of genuine reasons due to which the site gets navigated, please contact the support team.
  • On pressing the proceed button, the questions will be displayed and the participant has 50 minutes to complete the assessment.
  • On pressing the quit button, the participant will be redirected to the login page and the assessment attempt is not lost.
